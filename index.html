Meet Cody Croft

Cody Croft has always built the things other people only sketch on a napkin.

C3 Designs runs out of Bldg. 1 on FM 2164 in Sanger, Texas — a custom shop where Cody takes on the builds most shops pass on. The wild idea. The “can you actually do that?” project. The tired classic that deserves a second life with modern muscle underneath it.

He’s a hands-on custom builder, start to finish. Fabrication, bodywork, chassis, drivetrain — it all happens under one roof, run through one set of hands. That’s what keeps a build honest: the person who dreams it up with you is the same person turning the wrenches.

His whole philosophy is simple — build the crazy stuff he sees in his head, and make customers’ dreams come true while he’s at it. No production line. No shortcuts. No two builds the same.

Capabilities

What rolls out of Bldg. 1

From a tired classic to a clean-sheet build, C3 handles the whole car under one roof.

// 01

Full Custom Builds

Ground-up builds from your vision — chassis, body, drivetrain, interior. We start with the idea and end with a running, driving machine.

// 02

Restomods & Restorations

Classic looks, modern muscle. Keep the soul of a Chevelle or Mustang while it stops, steers, and runs like a car built this decade.

// 03

Metal Fabrication

Custom one-off metalwork — panels, brackets, mounts, mini-tubs. If it doesn’t exist off the shelf, we make it from scratch.

// 04

Suspension & Chassis

Get it sitting right and driving right. Suspension upgrades, chassis work, and stance dialed in for the street or the strip.

// 05

Engine & Driveline

Swaps, upgrades, and the wiring to back it up. More power, put down clean, built to actually live on the road.

// 06

Paint & Body

The part everyone sees. Bodywork and finish done patiently, so the panels are straight and the paint is deep.

How a build happens

From sketch to send-it

Every C3 build moves through the same five stages — so you always know where your car stands.

01

Vision

We sit down, talk the dream, and figure out what’s real, what’s wild, and what it takes.

02

Plan

Direction, parts, and a path. The build gets mapped before a single cut is made.

03

Fabricate

Metal, chassis, drivetrain. This is where the idea turns into a real machine.

04

Finish

Bodywork, paint, interior, and the details that separate good from done-right.

05

Reveal

Keys in hand. You drive away in the thing that started as a thought.
